About Me!

My name is Abigail Morse, and I'm a freelance editor.

I have a degree in Creative Writing with a minor in Technical Editing, which gives me a strong foundation in both storytelling and precision.

As a freelance editor, I work with self-published authors to refine their books at every stage—whether that's shaping the structure through developmental editing, improving clarity and flow at the line level, or polishing grammar and consistency. I combine creative insight with technical accuracy to help authors strengthen their manuscripts without losing their voice.

My approach is collaborative, thoughtful, and designed to support you through the publishing process.
